# Vendor Payout Policy - UtsavNest

## 💰 Payment Structure

### Platform Commission
- **5%** of total booking value
- Covers: Platform operations, marketing, payment processing, customer support
- Deducted from advance payment only

### Payment Split
- **30%** - Advance payment (paid by guest during booking)
- **70%** - Balance payment (collected by you at check-in)

---

## 📅 Payout Schedule

### Advance Payout (30% of booking)
| Detail | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **When** | **3 days before guest check-in** |
| **Amount** | Advance payment - 5% platform commission |
| **Timeline** | Processed automatically 3 days before check-in |
| **Method** | Admin-managed transfer to your bank |
| **Status** | Shows as "Pending" until 3 days before check-in |
| **Why this timing** | Aligns with cancellation policy for fair protection |

### Balance Collection (70% of booking)
| Detail | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **When** | At guest check-in |
| **Amount** | Full 70% (no deductions) |
| **Method** | Cash/UPI - Collect directly from guest |
| **Platform Fee** | None! You keep 100% of balance |

## 🚫 Cancellation Policy Impact (Aligned with Payout Timing)

### Understanding the 3-Day Payout Timing

The advance payout is strategically timed **3 days before check-in** to align perfectly with our cancellation policy. Here's how it protects both you and the guest:

| Cancellation Timing | Guest Gets | Vendor Gets | Reason |
|---------------------|------------|-------------|---------|
| **>3 days before check-in** (>72 hours) | 100% refund | ₹0 (no payout yet) | Payout not yet processed, easy 100% refund |
| **24-48 hours before** | 50% refund | 50% of advance | Payout already sent, fair 50-50 split |
| **<24 hours before** | 30% refund | 70% of advance | Last minute cancellation, vendor compensated |
| **Vendor rejection** | 100% refund | ₹0 | Vendor initiated, no payout made |

### Why This System is Fair:

✅ **For Guests:**
- Get full refund if they cancel more than 3 days ahead
- Pay reduced penalty (50%) if they cancel 1-2 days ahead
- Understand they lose the advance if they cancel last minute

✅ **For Vendors:**
- Don't receive advance too early (avoids refund complications)
- Get 50% compensation if guest cancels 1-2 days ahead
- Get 70% compensation if guest cancels last minute (<24h)

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do I receive the advance payout?**
A: 3-5 business days after you confirm the booking.

**Q: Is there a minimum payout amount?**
A: No, we process payouts for all confirmed bookings.

**Q: What if the guest doesn't pay the balance?**
A: Balance is collected at check-in. If guest doesn't check in, you still receive the advance payout.

**Q: Can I change my bank details?**
A: Yes, update anytime in the KYC/Payout Details section (requires password verification).

**Q: What happens if booking is cancelled?**
A: If cancelled before confirmation - no payout. If after - advance payout may be reversed.
